# The Framework in One Idea

The author has been influenced by the work of Sarah Winters and her team at Content Design London. Their work on Content Design has changed the author's approach, and their principles are applied here to thinking about how product teams may work alongside AI tooling.

The core move: point the discipline of content design inward, at the product manager's own work and the work of the teams they run.

> Content used to be something you read. Now it's something you use. Content design is about giving your audience what they want, when they need it, in the way they expect.
> — Sarah Winters

Most teams aim that at customers. This framework aims it at the product manager's own process: the research, the decisions, the specs, and the updates other teams depend on. Heavily guided AI tooling gathers the raw material and drafts the outputs. The person reviews, decides, and approves. Everything traces back to one shared record.
